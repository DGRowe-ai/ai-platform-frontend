Business Phone Number

This is your real business phone number. When customers call it, your phone provider forwards the call to your AI receptionist. We use this number to identify your business and load your personalized Voicebot settings (knowledge base, personality, business name, spelling name). Without this number, the Voicebot cannot determine which business the call belongs to.

Call Forwarding Setup

To activate your AI phone receptionist, forward your existing business phone number to your Rowe AI Voicebot number:

Forward all calls to: (+1) 416-367-0103

Customers will continue calling your usual number, but the AI receptionist will answer for you. Below are quick steps for common Canadian carriers:

Bell

  1. Dial *72
  2. Enter 416-367-0103
  3. Press Call

Rogers

  1. Dial *21*4163670103#
  2. Press Call

Telus

  1. Dial *72
  2. Enter 416-367-0103
  3. Press Call

Fido

  1. Dial *21*4163670103#
  2. Press Call

Koodo

  1. Dial *21*4163670103#
  2. Press Call

To disable forwarding: Dial ##21# and press Call.

Need help? Email support@roweai.ca or call 226-343-9977.

Suggested Prompt Example

Provide customers with a sample prompt showing how to write effective instructions for their voicebot.

Example Prompt: "You are the AI receptionist for my business. Greet callers politely and professionally. Answer questions about store hours, location, pricing, and services using the information I provide. If a caller asks to book an appointment, collect their name, date, and time, then confirm you will forward the request to the owner. If you don't know an answer, respond kindly and let the caller know you will pass their question to the business owner."

After editing knowledge or tone, click Save Settings before placing a test call. Also save your business phone number above and forward that line to the Voicebot number.
